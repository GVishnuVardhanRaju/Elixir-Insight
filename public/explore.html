Frequently Asked Questions

Is moderate alcohol consumption safe?

Moderate drinking may provide certain health benefits, but excessive consumption can be harmful.

Which drink has antioxidants?

Red wine contains antioxidants like resveratrol which may support heart health.

What is considered moderate drinking?

Generally one drink per day for women and two drinks per day for men.

Is alcohol good for stress relief?

Alcohol may temporarily relax the body but should not be used as a regular stress management method.
